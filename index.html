Frequently Asked Questions

What are your store hours?
We're open Monday to Saturday from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Sundays from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We're here to serve you at your convenience!
Do you offer home delivery?
Yes! We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2:00 PM within a 10km radius. Delivery fees apply based on distance. Free delivery on orders above KES 5,000!
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, M-PESA, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and all major mobile payment platforms. We also offer convenient payment on delivery options!
Can I return or exchange products?
Yes, we have a 7-day return policy for non-perishable items with receipt. Perishable items can be exchanged within 24 hours if there's a quality issue. Your satisfaction is our priority!
Do you have a loyalty program?
Absolutely! Join our SwiftMart Rewards program and earn points on every purchase. Points can be redeemed for discounts, and members get exclusive access to special deals!
How do I place an online order?
Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, and choose your preferred payment and delivery options. You'll receive a confirmation via SMS and email!
